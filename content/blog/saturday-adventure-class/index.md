\    Do you have someone in your family who enjoys spending time in the great outdoors and wants to grow their survival skills? We are excited to announce an opportunity for your child to learn basic outdoor survival skills here at Royal Ridges! This six-week course is designed especially for grades 4 - 8 and will include instruction on building a fire, shelter building, navigation, plant safety, and more. These 2-hour classes every Saturday are a great way to start the weekend, and many of our participants have enjoyed learning and growing their skills. The new session starts on April 23.
